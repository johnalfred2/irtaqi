Privacy Policy

Irtaqi does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Quran page images are cached via the Service Worker for offline reading.
  • Reading progress (which words/ayahs you've revealed) is stored in your browser's localStorage.
  • Theme preference and language selection are also stored in localStorage.

None of this data is sent to any server. It remains on your device and can be cleared at any time via your browser or app settings.

Internet Access

The app only connects to the internet to download Quran page images and check for updates. These connections are made directly to GitHub's servers (raw.githubusercontent.com) or your configured source. No analytics, tracking, or advertising scripts are used.

Third Parties

Irtaqi does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer at the repository: codeberg.org/johnalfred2/Irtaqi
